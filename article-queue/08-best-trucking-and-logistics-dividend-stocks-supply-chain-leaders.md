**A:** Most transportation & logistics companies maintain payout ratios between 30-60% of earnings, balancing current income with growth investment. Higher payout ratios may limit dividend growth but provide more income.

**A:** Consider diversifying by company size (large-cap, mid-cap), business segments, and geographic exposure. A mix of 4-6 holdings or an ETF provides good diversification while remaining manageable.

**A:** Most transportation & logistics company dividends qualify as qualified dividends, taxed at preferential capital gains rates (15-20% for most investors). Some specialized entities (MLPs, REITs) have different tax treatment. Consult a tax professional.
