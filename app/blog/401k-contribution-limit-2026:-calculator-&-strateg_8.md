### 2. Contribution Timing Strategies

#### Front-Loading vs. Dollar Cost Averaging

**Front-Loading Benefits:**
- Earlier market exposure
- Guaranteed maximum contribution
- Potential tax advantages

**Dollar Cost Averaging Benefits:**
- Consistent investing throughout the year
- Reduced impact of market volatility
- Easier budgeting

### 3. Tax Planning Considerations

#### Traditional vs. Roth 401k Options

**Traditional 401k:**
- Immediate tax deduction
- Tax-deferred growth
- Higher take-home pay

**Roth 401k:**
- After-tax contributions
- Tax-free withdrawals in retirement
- No required minimum distributions
