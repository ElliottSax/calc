### Projected 401k Limits for 2026

Based on current inflation trends and historical increases, here are the anticipated limits:

- Employee contribution limit: $24,000 (standard)

Use this simple formula to determine your potential savings:

### 2. Employer Match Optimization

Most common employer match formulas:
- 100% match on first 3-6% of salary
- 50% match up to 6% of salary

#### Example Match Calculation:
```
Salary: $80,000
Employer Match: 100% up to 5%
Maximum Match: $80,000 × 5% = $4,000
Required Employee Contribution: $4,000
```
