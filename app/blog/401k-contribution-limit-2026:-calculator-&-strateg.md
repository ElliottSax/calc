## Strategic Planning for 2026 Contributions

### 1. Front-Loading vs. Dollar-Cost Averaging

#### Front-Loading Strategy
- Contribute higher amounts early in the year
- Benefits: Earlier market exposure, guaranteed max contribution
- Example: Contributing $4,000 monthly for first 6 months

#### Dollar-Cost Averaging Strategy
- Equal contributions throughout the year
- Benefits: Consistent budgeting, reduced market timing risk
- Example: $2,000 monthly for 12 months

### 2. Employer Match Optimization

Most employers offer a matching contribution, typically structured as:
- 50% match up to 6% of salary
- 100% match up to 3-4% of salary

### 1. Investment Allocation Strategy

Recommended allocation based on age:
- 20s-30s: 90% stocks, 10% bonds
- 40s: 80% stocks, 20% bonds
- 50s: 70% stocks, 30% bonds
- 60s: 60% stocks, 40% bonds
