## Moving Your Dividends

### How to Transfer Accounts

If you already have dividends elsewhere:

1. **Request Transfer Out** (ACAT - Automated Customer Account Transfer)
   - New broker handles paperwork
   - Usually free
   - Takes 3-5 business days
   - No trading during transfer

2. **Benefits of Transferring**
   - Consolidate holdings
   - Better tools/research
   - Lower fees (if applicable)
   - Simplified management

3. **Avoid Transferring**
   - If you have unrealized losses (sell instead, harvest loss)
   - If within 30 days of dividend payment
   - If holding beneficial positions
   - Minimal accounts <$1,000
