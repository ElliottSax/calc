## FAQ

**Q: When should I start investing?**
A: Once you maintain 3-6 months in emergency savings, move excess to investments.

**Q: How do I reinvest dividends without fees?**
A: Most brokerages offer free DRIP programs.

**Q: Should I buy stocks or ETFs?**
A: ETFs offer diversification. Use 70% ETFs, 30% individual stocks.

**Q: Expected annual return?**
A: Conservative (60/40): 5-6%. Dividend-focused: 2.5-4.5%.

**Q: What if market drops 20%?**
