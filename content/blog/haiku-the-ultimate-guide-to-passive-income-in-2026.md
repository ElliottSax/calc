## FAQ

**Q: What's the safest passive income source?**
A: Treasury bonds (government guaranteed) and dividend aristocrats (60+ years dividend growth). Both offer safety over yield percentage.

**Q: Should I chase high yields?**
A: No. Yields above 5% often indicate risk. Stick with 2-4% from quality sources. Higher risk isn't worth slightly higher income in passive strategies.

**Q: Can I live on passive income?**
A: With sufficient portfolio size, yes. $3M at 4% yield = $120,000 annually. Most people reach this by 55-60 through consistent saving and compounding.

**Q: Should I take dividends or reinvest?**
A: Reinvest during accumulation phase (building portfolio). Take dividends only during retirement (need income). Reinvestment compounds to exponentially larger income.

**Q: How do I adjust for inflation?**
A: Focus on dividend growth stocks (growing earnings offset inflation). Rebalance annually to refresh yields.
