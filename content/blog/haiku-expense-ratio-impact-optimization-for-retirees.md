# Expense Ratio Impact Optimization for Retirees

Expense ratios fundamentally impact long-term investment returns. For retired investors taking withdrawals, understanding and minimizing expense ratios separates millionaires from average investors. This guide reveals expense ratio impact and optimization strategies specific to retirees.

## Understanding Expense Ratios

Expense ratios represent annual costs charged by funds, expressed as percentage of assets under management. A fund with $100 billion assets and 0.05% ratio charges $50 million annually (0.05% of $100B).

**Example Impact:**
- $100,000 invested at 0.05% ratio = $50 yearly cost
- $100,000 invested at 0.50% ratio = $500 yearly cost
- Difference: $450 annually = compounding loss
