## Understanding Dividend Yield for Sector ETFs

Dividend yield varies dramatically across sector ETFs. Defensive sectors like utilities and consumer staples yield higher; technology and discretionary sectors yield lower. Understanding sector yields guides allocation decisions.

## The Dividend Yield Formula

**Dividend Yield = (Annual Dividends / Current ETF Price) × 100**

Sector ETFs distribute whatever their companies pay, creating sector-specific yield profiles.

## Worked Example

- ETF Price: $120/share
- Annual Distribution: $2.40/share
- Calculation: ($2.40 / $120) × 100 = **2.0%**

| Sector | Key Factors | Characteristics |

| Healthcare | Growth + income | Moderate yields |

| Finance | Capital strength | Variable yields |

## Step-by-Step Yield Calculation

1. Identify sector ETF and current price
2. Gather 12-month distribution history
3. Sum all distributions
4. Divide by current price
5. Compare across sectors

## Sector Rotation and Yield

Economic cycles affect sector yield leadership:

- **Expansion**: Tech/discretionary rise (lower yield)
- **Maturity**: Utilities/staples outperform (higher yield)
- **Contraction**: Defensive sectors lead (high yield)
- **Recovery**: Cyclicals accelerate (variable yield)

## Diversification Across Sectors

Broad sector diversification provides:
- Different yield profiles
- Non-correlated returns
- Economic cycle protection
- Reduced concentration

## FAQ

**Q: Which sector ETF has highest yield?**
A: Currently utilities and energy, but this fluctuates with rates and commodity prices.

**Q: Should I overweight high-yield sectors?**
A: No. High yield may reflect slower growth. Balance yield with growth potential.

**Q: How does sector rotation affect ETF selection?**
A: Diversify across sectors rather than concentrating on current high-yield leaders.

**Q: Can sector yields turn negative?**
A: No, but dividends can be cut if conditions deteriorate.

**Q: How often do sector ETFs rebalance?**
A: Most track indices that rebalance quarterly or annually.

## Conclusion

Understanding dividend yield across sectors enables strategic allocation. Recognize which sectors provide income (utilities, staples) and which offer growth (technology) to construct a balanced dividend portfolio resilient across economic cycles.
