# Norfolk Southern Dividend: Rail Transport Income Stock

Norfolk Southern Corporation (NSC) operates the premier freight railroad network in the Eastern United States, delivering consistent dividends backed by monopolistic routes and intermodal freight growth. With 20+ consecutive years of dividend increases and improving operational efficiency, Norfolk Southern offers reliable transportation sector income with growth potential.

## Key Takeaways

- Norfolk Southern has increased dividends for 23 consecutive years—approaching Dividend Aristocrat milestone
- Current yield of 2.0-2.2% backed by irreplaceable Eastern rail network
- Payout ratio of 40-44% provides balance between income and network investment
- Free cash flow of $3.5-4.5 billion supports dividends and capital improvements
- TOP|SPG (operational improvement program) drives margin expansion and dividend growth
