## Analysis
When evaluating dividend stocks under $50, it's essential to consider several key metrics. The current yield, which is the annual dividend payment divided by the stock price, is a critical factor in determining the attractiveness of a dividend stock. Additionally, the payout ratio, which is the percentage of earnings paid out as dividends, should be reasonable to ensure the sustainability of the dividend payments. Finally, the growth rate of the dividend payments is crucial in determining the potential for long-term wealth creation.
