A: Both work:

Q: What if a dividend aristocrat cuts their dividend?
A: Extremely rare (hasn't happened in 50+ years for mega-cap aristocrats), but mitigated:

- Company still grows capital otherwise

Q: How often should I rebalance an aristocrat portfolio?
A: Annually only:
- Let dividends compound

- Avoid tax-creating trading
