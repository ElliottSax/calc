## Advanced Portfolio Techniques

**Diversification Across Asset Classes:**
Modern portfolio theory demonstrates that combining uncorrelated assets reduces volatility without reducing returns.

| Portfolio | Allocation | Return | Volatility | Sharpe |

## Implementation Specifics

**For Self-Employed Workers:**

- Tax advantage: Deduct all contributions from taxable income

## FAQ

**Q: Should self-employed workers prioritize SEP-IRA or Solo 401k?**
A: Solo 401k allows higher contributions ($69,000+) and offers loan provisions. Best choice for most self-employed.

**Q: Can I contribute to both traditional and Roth IRAs?**
A: Yes, but total contributions across both cannot exceed $7,000 annually. Consider backdoor Roth if income exceeds limits.

**Q: How much should I allocate to growth vs. income?**
A: Under age 50: 80-90% growth, 10-20% income. Age 50-65: 60-70% growth, 30-40% income. Over 65: 40-50% growth, 50-60% income.

**Q: What's the maximum safe withdrawal rate?**
A: 3-4% annually. 4% provides 95% success rate over 30-year retirement. Anything above 5% creates sequence-of-returns risk.
