## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Are dividend ETFs better than growth ETFs?**
A: Different purposes. Dividend ETFs = income focus. Growth ETFs = capital appreciation. Balanced combines both.

**Q: Should I reinvest dividends or take as income?**
A: Young (<50): Reinvest. Near retirement: Take half, reinvest half. Retired: Take as income.

**Q: Which dividend ETF is safest?**
A: SCHD (dividend growers, strong quality). VYM (diversified high dividend). Avoid JEPI (concentrated, complex).

**Q: Can I live on dividend income alone?**
A: Yes, after 20+ years of accumulation. SCHD dividend growth means distributions eventually exceed portfolio needs.
