## FAQ

**Q: How often should I rebalance?**
A: Annual rebalancing works well. Rebalance sooner if drift exceeds 5% from targets.

**Q: What's the optimal stock/bond split for my age?**
A: Use 110 minus your age for stock percentage. A 35-year-old targets 75% stocks.

**Q: Should I buy individual stocks or ETFs?**
A: ETFs offer instant diversification, lower costs, and better tax efficiency.

**Q: How do I handle required minimum distributions?**
A: RMDs begin at age 73. Calculate: Account balance divided by life expectancy factor.

**Q: What size emergency fund do I need?**
A: Maintain 6-12 months of expenses in liquid accounts separate from investments.

**Q: Can I automate my investing?**
A: Yes, set up automatic monthly transfers for consistent dollar-cost averaging.
