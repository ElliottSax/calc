## FAQ

**Q: What is the benchmark for this strategy?**
A: Use the appropriate index (S&P 500, MSCI EAFE, Bloomberg Bond Index) as your comparison baseline.

**Q: How often should I rebalance?**
A: Annually, or when allocations drift >2-3% from target. Excessive rebalancing creates tax drag.

**Q: Can I improve returns by active trading?**
A: Data shows 80-90% of active traders underperform buy-and-hold after fees and taxes.

**Q: Should I use limit orders or market orders?**
A: Market orders for small amounts <$25,000. Limit orders for larger positions to control execution prices.
