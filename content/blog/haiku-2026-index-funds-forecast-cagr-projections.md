## Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR)

CAGR measures annual growth over multiple years, accounting for compounding.

**CAGR = (Ending Value / Beginning Value)^(1/Years) - 1**

### Real Example

$10,000 investment with volatility:
- Year 1: +15% = $11,500
- Year 2: -8% = $10,580
- Year 3: +22% = $12,908

CAGR = ($12,908 / $10,000)^(1/3) - 1 = 8.78%
