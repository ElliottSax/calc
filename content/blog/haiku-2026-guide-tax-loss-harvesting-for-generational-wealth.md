## Introduction

For generational wealth spanning 50-90 years and multiple family members, tax-loss harvesting becomes increasingly critical. A saved $5,000-$10,000 in taxes compounded over 70 years becomes $200,000+ in additional family wealth, making systematic harvesting essential for generational portfolios.

## Multi-Generational Tax Strategies

### The "Step-Up in Basis" Advantage

Upon death, inherited positions receive stepped-up basis (eliminating capital gains taxes). However, while alive, harvesting saves taxes:

Example: Generation 1 founder

Years 1-30 (Building to $1M):
- Annual harvesting: $25,000-$35,000
- Annual tax benefit: $6,000-$8,400
- 30-year tax savings: $180,000-$252,000

At death: Assets transfer with stepped-up basis
- No capital gains taxes owed on inherited gains
- Heirs receive $1M+ tax-free
- Generation 1 saved $180,000-$252,000 during lifetime

The combination of lifetime harvesting + step-up basis provides maximum tax efficiency.

## Harvesting Strategy for Trusts

For dynasty trusts holding $5M-$20M:
- Harvest losses at trust level
- Offset trust's income (ordinary income from bonds, dividends)
- Trust-level tax rate often 37% (highest bracket)
- Tax benefit: Losses offset at 37% vs. 20% individual rate

Example: $50,000 loss
- At individual 20% rate: $10,000 tax benefit
- At trust 37% rate: $18,500 tax benefit
- Advantage: $8,500 additional savings per harvest

Over 50 years: Multiplied harvests = $100,000+ additional tax savings.

## FAQ

Q: How long should I maintain harvesting discipline?
A: Perpetually. If trust is true dynasty (100+ year duration), harvesting compounds indefinitely.

Q: Should I harvest losses in taxable or tax-deferred accounts?
A: Only taxable accounts. Tax-deferred (401k, IRA) trading isn't taxable; harvesting serves no purpose there.

Q: What's maximum annual harvesting without IRS challenges?
A: No legal limit. IRS only cares:
1. Aren't buying substantially identical security within 30 days (wash sale)
2. Not creating "abusive" patterns claiming losses on identical-cost securities

Normal harvesting is never challenged.

Q: How much effort for $10M generational portfolio?
A: 20-40 hours/year (1-2 hours/week):
- Monitor positions monthly
- Execute harvests quarterly
- Document transactions
- Coordinate with tax advisors

Effort is manageable for the $100,000-$500,000 in annual tax benefits generated.
