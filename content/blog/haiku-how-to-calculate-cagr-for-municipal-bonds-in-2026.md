# How to Calculate CAGR for Municipal Bonds in 2026

Municipal bonds (munis) complicate CAGR calculation because returns are quoted on a tax-equivalent basis. Understanding how to calculate true CAGR reveals whether tax-free returns justify lower yields.

## Municipal Bond CAGR Fundamentals

CAGR formula:


Munis require tax-adjustment comparison to taxable bonds.

## Real Example: VTEB

Vanguard Intermediate Tax-Exempt (VTEB), 2015-2025:
- Starting price: 8.50
- Ending price: 2.30
- Cumulative distributions: 2.40 per share

CAGR = (2.30 + 2.40) / 8.50)^(1/10) - 1 = 2.94%

VTEB delivered 2.94% CAGR tax-free.

## Tax-Equivalent CAGR Comparison

For 37% marginal bracket earner:

Muni CAGR: 2.94% (tax-free)
Equivalent taxable yield: 2.94% / (1 - 0.37) = 4.67%

Compare to:
- Treasuries: 3.45% after-tax = 2.17%
- Corporates: 3.80% after-tax = 2.39%
- Munis: 2.94% after-tax = 2.94% (winner)

## Municipal Bond Types

## FAQ

**Q: Are munis safe?**
A: General obligation bonds are safe. Revenue bonds carry default risk.
